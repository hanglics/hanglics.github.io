Biography

Bio


My name is Hang Li, currently I am a PhD student at the University of Queensland, Australia, supervised by Dr. Guido Zuccon. I was graduated from the University of Minnesota Twin-Cities with my Bachelor of Science degree in the United States.

For my PhD study, I am researching on how to improve the agricultural QA systems with Information Retrieval. My research focuses on developing new methods and machine learning models to assist farmers make accurate growing decisions.
